About QR Code Generator Pro

This tool helps users generate QR codes instantly for free. It's lightweight, privacy-minded, and runs entirely in your browser — no server required.

Why use this tool?

  • Instant QR code generation from text or URLs
  • Customizable colors and sizes
  • Download high-resolution PNG images suitable for print
  • History stored locally in your browser

How it works

We use a small, open-source QR encoding library to render QR images on a canvas element. All generation and storage happen locally in your browser.
